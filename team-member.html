Wantoe T. Wantoe's Background
ScholarlyPath, known for his advocacy for women and children's rights, combining intellect and activism to drive change. He holds a Master’s Degree in Public Policy from the University of Oxford and has been pivotal in shaping national narratives as the 2023 National Orator of Liberia's Flag Day. His experience includes working with Congressman Jamaal Bowman in the U.S., where he contributed to significant legislation. Wantoe serves as a Board of Trustee member of AFS Intercultural Programs, the largest intercultural education organization worldwide. He is also pursuing his second master's degree at the University of Oxford, focusing on Contemporary Education. His global recognition includes accolades from the Princess Diana Award and the Obama Foundation, and he is a member of the United Nations Department of Global Communication Youth Steering Committee. At ScholarlyPath, his leadership emphasizes education, sustainability, and youth empowerment.
