Welcome Message From The CEO

As a Liberian from an underprivileged background, I understand the transformative power of education firsthand. My father's salary couldn't afford private university education in Liberia, yet pursuing scholarships and studying abroad shaped who I am today. It gave me the opportunity to study in the United States and earn my first Master’s in Public Policy at the University of Oxford. Now, I’m pursuing a second master's in Contemporary Education at Oxford on a full scholarship.

ScholarlyPath was founded by successful study abroad alumni with stories like mine, aiming to inspire and support others in realizing their dreams. Our flagship Study Abroad Symposium empowers Liberians by teaching them how to apply for global admissions and scholarship programs. Through storytelling and skill-building presentations, we expand knowledge on various application opportunities.

This Fall 2025, thousands of Liberians will pursue admission and scholarships. Our summit will empower and inspire them to be competitive and successful, serving as a platform for finding diverse scholarships and admission opportunities. We’ve seen this thrive in Ghana, Kenya, Nigeria, and across Africa. For the past three years, we’ve inspired many with this platform.

We believe you can join this revolution. Partner with us to help promote global opportunities for Liberians. Our mission is to support and inspire them through our journey and the experiences of others. This will be our primary focus and energy until the month ends.

Our summit is designed to empower you to apply for competitive full scholarships independently. Here’s what we aim to achieve:

  • Acquire Comprehensive Knowledge: Learn everything you need to know about studying abroad and develop the skills to navigate the application process independently.
  • Personalized Mentorship: Benefit from tailored guidance and support from mentors who align with your educational goals.
  • Empowerment for the Next Stage: Gain the tools and information necessary to advance in your study abroad journey.
  • In-Depth Scholarship Insights: Understand available scholarships and how to craft a competitive application to boost your chances of success.

The summit takes place just before the competitive application season, making it the perfect time to start your journey. We are excited about this edition, especially since we’ve partnered with a renowned institution to enhance the event. Secure your seat as soon as possible and don’t miss this opportunity!

If you want to partner or sponsor, please don’t hesitate to reach out.

Warm regards,

Wantoe T. Wantoe

Welcome to the Summit

The National Study Abroad Summit is an initiative aimed at creating international educational opportunities for Liberian students. This summit focuses on empowering students with the knowledge, tools, and networks necessary to pursue academic success abroad.

Join us for a two-day event featuring expert speakers, interactive workshops, and valuable networking opportunities. Our goal is to help students navigate the study abroad process with confidence and gain the insights needed to succeed in their international educational journey.

CEO’s Personal Story

As a proud Liberian who studied abroad, it was one of the most transformative experiences of my life. The exposure to new ideas and academic excellence abroad shaped my career and allowed me to bring skills and knowledge back to Liberia. The challenges and triumphs I encountered during my studies have inspired me to create this summit.

Vision for the Summit

This summit is designed to create similar pathways for Liberian students, equipping them with the tools they need to succeed internationally and contribute to Liberia’s development. We aim to provide practical advice, personal mentorship, and access to a network of professionals who can guide and support students in their study abroad journey.

Key Objectives

Acquire Comprehensive Knowledge

Learn everything you need to know about studying abroad, including application processes, visa requirements, and adapting to a new educational environment. Develop the skills to navigate the process independently with confidence.

Personalized Mentorship

Benefit from tailored guidance and support from mentors who align with your educational goals. Our experienced mentors will offer one-on-one sessions to address your specific needs and provide advice on how to achieve your academic and career objectives.

Empowerment for the Next Stage

Gain the tools and information necessary to advance in your study abroad journey. Learn how to effectively prepare for life abroad, manage your studies, and integrate into a new academic and cultural environment.

In-Depth Scholarship Insights

Understand available scholarships and how to craft a competitive application to boost your chances of success. Our sessions will cover various scholarship opportunities, application tips, and how to present yourself as a strong candidate.
