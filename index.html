About the Summit

The National Study Abroad Summit is Liberia's leading event for connecting students with global educational opportunities. It provides insights into higher education abroad, expert guidance on application processes, and access to scholarship programs.

Why Attend?

  • Discover opportunities for studying in prestigious international institutions.
  • Learn the ins and outs of scholarship applications and funding options.
  • Network with education professionals, students, and international university representatives.

Welcome from the CEO

As someone who has personally experienced the transformative power of international education, I want to ensure that every Liberian student has the chance to access similar opportunities. This summit represents a crucial step toward making global education accessible to all Liberians, regardless of their background.

Our Vision

The summit is designed to empower you to achieve your academic dreams by providing the tools, resources, and networks necessary for success.

Key Objectives

Global Education

Explore opportunities for studying in world-class universities and institutions.

Scholarship Guidance

Receive expert advice on applying for scholarships and financial aid.

Career Networking

Build connections with professionals and students who share your academic interests.
