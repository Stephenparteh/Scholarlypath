Welcome to the Summit

The National Study Abroad Summit is an initiative aimed at creating international educational opportunities for Liberian students. This summit focuses on empowering students with the knowledge, tools, and networks necessary to pursue academic success abroad.

Join us for a two-day event featuring expert speakers, interactive workshops, and valuable networking opportunities. Our goal is to help students navigate the study abroad process with confidence and gain the insights needed to succeed in their international educational journey.

CEO’s Personal Story

As a proud Liberian who studied abroad, it was one of the most transformative experiences of my life. The exposure to new ideas and academic excellence abroad shaped my career and allowed me to bring skills and knowledge back to Liberia. The challenges and triumphs I encountered during my studies have inspired me to create this summit.

Vision for the Summit

This summit is designed to create similar pathways for Liberian students, equipping them with the tools they need to succeed internationally and contribute to Liberia’s development. We aim to provide practical advice, personal mentorship, and access to a network of professionals who can guide and support students in their study abroad journey.

Key Objectives

Acquire Comprehensive Knowledge

Learn everything you need to know about studying abroad, including application processes, visa requirements, and adapting to a new educational environment. Develop the skills to navigate the process independently with confidence.

Personalized Mentorship

Benefit from tailored guidance and support from mentors who align with your educational goals. Our experienced mentors will offer one-on-one sessions to address your specific needs and provide advice on how to achieve your academic and career objectives.

Empowerment for the Next Stage

Gain the tools and information necessary to advance in your study abroad journey. Learn how to effectively prepare for life abroad, manage your studies, and integrate into a new academic and cultural environment.

In-Depth Scholarship Insights

Understand available scholarships and how to craft a competitive application to boost your chances of success. Our sessions will cover various scholarship opportunities, application tips, and how to present yourself as a strong candidate.
