About the Summit

Our summit will bring together 850 participants, reflecting the rich diversity of Liberia. Attendees will include students from various ethnic backgrounds, political affiliations, and educational experiences, all united by the common goal of pursuing academic excellence abroad.
This diverse group will comprise undergraduate, master's, and Ph.D. candidates interested in studying overseas. Additionally, we welcome professionals seeking opportunities for career advancement through international education. This inclusive approach ensures a wide range of perspectives and fosters a collaborative environment for learning and growth.

Purpose

Scholarly Path, in collaboration with the Center for African Policy, proudly presents the 4th Edition of the National Study Abroad Summit. This dynamic two-day event is designed to empower Liberian students who aspire to further their education through graduate and undergraduate study abroad programs.
Our mission is to provide participants with the tools, resources, and guidance needed to navigate the complex landscape of international education, unlocking pathways to global opportunities and academic excellence
