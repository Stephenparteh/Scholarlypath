About the Summit

Our summit will include 850 participants, representing the rich diversity of Liberia. Attendees will include students from various ethnic backgrounds, political affiliations, and educational experiences, all united by the common goal of pursuing academic excellence abroad.
This diverse group will comprise undergraduate, master's, and Ph.D. candidates interested in studying overseas. In addition, we welcome professionals seeking opportunities for career advancementthrough international education.This inclusive approach ensures a wide range ofperspectives and fosters a collaborative environmentfor learningand growth

Purpose

Scholarly Path, in collaboration with the Center for African Policy, proudly presents the 4th Edition of the National Study Abroad Summit. This dynamic two-day event is designed to empower Liberian students who aspire to further their education through graduate and undergraduate study abroad programs.
Our mission is to provide participants with the tools, resources, and guidance needed to navigate the complex landscape of international education, unlocking pathways to global opportunities and academic excellence

Why Attend Our Event?

Join us to explore a world of possibilities and take advantage of the following benefits.

Expert Guidance

Gain access to expert advice on navigating college applications, scholarship opportunities, and selecting suitable programs. Our team of experienced professionals will provide comprehensive support to help you make informed decisions about your academic journey.

Skills Training

Participate in interactive workshops designed to enhance your skills and prepare you for successful college admissions. Topics covered will include essay writing, interview preparation, standardized test strategies, and more.

Personalized Mentorship

Benefit from one-on-one mentoring sessions with seasoned educators and study abroad alumni. These personalized interactions will offer tailored advice, help you identify your strengths, and guide you toward achieving your academic and career goals.

Extensive Research Assistance

Access a wealth of information, including curated lists of colleges, universities, and scholarships across the US, Canada, Asia, the UK, and Europe. Our resources will empower you to explore a wide range of educational opportunities and choose the best fit for your aspirations.

Inspiring Alumni Stories

Hear firsthand accounts from study abroad alumni who have excelled on the global stage. Learn from their experiences, gain insights into overcoming challenges, and be inspired by their journeys of personal and academic growth.

Networking Opportunities

Connect with educational professionals, policymakers, and fellow students who share your passion for learning and exploration. Build valuable relationships that can support your academic endeavors and future career prospects.

Career Enhancement

Discover how international education can significantly enhance your career opportunities. Learn about global job markets, develop cross-cultural competencies, and understand how to leverage your study abroad experience to stand out in a competitive workforce.

Cultural Immersion

Engage in cultural exchanges that promote global understanding and unity. Experience diverse perspectives, broaden your worldview, and develop a deeper appreciation for different cultures and traditions.
